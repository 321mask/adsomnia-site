A lucid dream is the moment you realize you're dreaming — while you're still asleep. From there, the dream is yours to explore.

How it works

Three steps, every night.

Step 01

Drift off

Start a session before bed and fall asleep to calming, sleep-friendly audio. A short pre-sleep exercise primes your mind for awareness.

Step 02

Sense the cue

Through the night, subtle Apple Watch vibrations are timed to your sleep — gentle nudges meant to reach you in the dream without waking you.

Step 03

Go lucid

You recognize the signal inside the dream and awareness clicks on. Now you can fly, explore, or simply look around a world made of your own mind.

Inside the app

Built to make lucidity repeatable.

Lucid dreaming is a skill. Ad Somnia gives you the tools to practice it night after night.

Gentle sleep onset

Calming audio eases you down into sleep so your session starts the moment you're ready to rest.

Smart Watch cues

Haptic cues fire in timed windows through the night, calibrated to be felt in the dream, not in the waking world.

Awareness training

Short nightly pre-sleep sessions build the habit of noticing — the core reflex behind every lucid dream.

Session graph

View the night's curve to see when your cue windows landed and how your sessions evolve over time.

Cue tracking

Each session counts the cues delivered, so you can fine-tune timing and strength to what works for you.

Private by design

Sleep data from your Apple Watch is read through HealthKit and used on your device. It's never sold or shared.
